WHAT WE DO

Why we collect?

Kenya is importing yearly more than 100 million kg's of clothes. After use all these textiles and foot wear end up being burnt or littered. By collecting the items clean and dry we give them a 2nd,3rd,4th life! We can prepare for re-use or recycling. this way we set up a circular economy for textiles and shoes that generate jobs and funds for charity.

How we collect ?

ACT places collection containers in shopping malls, churches, schools and universities. Through the collection our collection partners generate funds for their charity causes.

what we do with it ?

Every item is sorted and carefully evaluated. The items are made ready for re-wear or recycling. wearable items flow back to the second-hand market or are donated to orphanages and elderly homes. Recyclable materials are processed into shoes, blankets, bags, furniture, art, and eventually new clothes! Check our shop for homemade items!
